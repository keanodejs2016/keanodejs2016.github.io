How To Create A Lynda.com Account

tags: Lynda.com, Free Account

Be at KEA, Lygten 37.
Sign in to the network (like you normally do)
Go to: http://iplogin.lynda.com/
Sign up using your KEA-mail and password.
Have fun learning!
At this stage you can log in to Lynda.com from any network (from home e.g.).
