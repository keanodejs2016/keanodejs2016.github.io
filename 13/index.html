Day 1 - Introduction

tags: Day 1, Node.js, Getting Started

Time: 5/2-2016, 08:30 - 13:20

In this first class you will get introduced to what we are going to work on the next 14 weeks, including a presentation of the two mandatory assignments in this elective. You will also get an introduction to the Node.js server, you will download and install the server, and you will create your first webserver and webpage. This webserver will run locally on your computer and it will be accessable on your local network for everybody. You will then deploy your server and site to an online host, (Azure), and end up by having made your first simple website accessable to everyone.

Objectives:

  • An overview of what will be covered in the next 16 weeks.
    • Curriculum
    • Exam and Mandatory Assignments
  • Introduction to and installation of Node.js.
  • Make your first Webserver and a Hello World web page .
